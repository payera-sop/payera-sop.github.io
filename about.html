Our Story

Sun & Wick was born from a love of nature, calm spaces, and intentional living. Each candle is hand-poured using sustainable soy wax, cotton wicks, and fragrance blends inspired by the world around us.

We believe in creating warmth, balance, and serenity through scent. Every purchase supports small-batch craftsmanship and eco-conscious materials.
